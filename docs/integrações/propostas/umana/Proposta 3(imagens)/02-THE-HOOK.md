# SLIDE 02 - THE HOOK (GATILHO DE ATENÇÃO)

## 📋 Especificações Técnicas
- **Formato:** 1920x1080px (16:9)
- **Estilo:** Minimalista, impactante, emocional
- **Objetivo:** Quebrar padrão e gerar curiosidade

---

## 🎨 PROMPT PARA GERAÇÃO

```
Create a minimalist impact slide with emotional hook, 1920x1080px.

LAYOUT:
- Centered text with massive white space
- Light background with subtle gradient
- UzzAi mascot on the right side waving friendly
- Breadcrumb at bottom

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Main quote: "Imagine nunca perder um cliente por falta de resposta rápida." in Poppins SemiBold, very large (60-70pt), #1C1C1C (black)
- Second line: "Agora é possível." in Poppins SemiBold, same size, #1ABC9C (mint green) for emphasis
- Breadcrumb: "/ PROPOSTA COMERCIAL / O DESAFIO" in Inter Regular, small, #B0B0B0

COLOR PALETTE:
- Background: White (#FFFFFF) with subtle gradient to #1ABC9C (5% opacity)
- Main text: #1C1C1C (black)
- Emphasis text: #1ABC9C (mint green)
- Breadcrumb: #B0B0B0 (silver)

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- UzzAi mascot (eye with petals and articulated body) on right side, waving or in friendly pose
- Mascot colors: #1ABC9C (mint green), #1C1C1C (black), #FFD700 (gold accent)
- Large decorative quotation marks in #1ABC9C with low opacity
- Minimal, clean design with focus on the message
- Lots of negative space for dramatic effect

STYLE:
- Minimalist and clean
- Emotional and impactful
- High contrast typography
- Friendly mascot adds personality
- Professional yet approachable
```
